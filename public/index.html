Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about SmartEDU LMS.

What is SmartEDU LMS?

SmartEDU LMS is a learning management system that combines structured lessons, guided projects, and AI-assisted tools.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress, badges, and certificates are stored locally after you log in, so you can pick up where you left off.

Do I need an account to use the AI tools?

You can explore the AI tools right away, but logging in helps you save progress and keep your work organized.

Is SmartEDU LMS suitable for teams?

The admin studio is designed to support structured content management, making it useful for individuals and small teams.
